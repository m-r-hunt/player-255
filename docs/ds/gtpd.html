Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective

  • Complete on 2023-04-27

  • 5 / 5

  • Release Date: Jan 11, 2011

  • Meta Score: 83

Screenshots

Notes

This game is really good. It's made by the Phoenix Wright people and it shows (in a good way).


Unique gameplay, kind of a point and click adventure but with a unique mechanic for manipulating the environment and a time loop mechanic that seems to predate the recent trend in that direction.


Great story that sucked me in towards the end.


Really nice graphics, looks great on DS.


Only criticisms: sometimes a little wordy, some puzzles a little hard to solve logically like a lot of point and click games.


It's a shame this game didn't get more attention.
