Screenshots

Notes

JRPG with an interesting set up - you're trying to save the world by time travelling into alternate timelines to find the one where you can save everything.


Has an interesting battle system, but I think this kind of ended up working against it in a strange way, since you can't turn off your brain and mash through random encounters, but there are a lot of random encounters.


I tried self-experimenting by playing this on a full 2x speed, and I think maybe I liked it more because of that, but it didn't boost it enough for me to actually get through it.
