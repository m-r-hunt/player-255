Notes

Blue Dragon Plus is a sequel to a fairly medium JRPG that came out on the Xbox 360. However, it's a real time strategy game with RPG elements. It's one of the lower rated games that I put in out of curiosity.


It's part of a little subgroup of touch screen RTSes that popped up on the DS. It's kind of fine, but boring. You control a few guys, they walk slowly around and slap enemies. Controls are a bit awkward.


Interesting to give it a go but I can't see myself finishing it.
