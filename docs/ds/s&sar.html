Notes

Solid Mario Kart-alike. Good graphics and track visuals. Slightly jank gameplay. A few places I felt like the correct route wasn't signposted well and I just stacked it into a wall the first lap or two.


There was one course that was really hard, like all 90 corners and u turns. Even the power up that blast you fast forward when you're at the back (e.g. like the bullet bill in mario) was unable to navigate the course.


Completed all the cups (on easy) and saw credits.
