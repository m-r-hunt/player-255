Notes

Art Academy is a drawing program with art lessons built in. I included it, not because its a game I wanted to complete but because it's an iconic DS title I wanted to have a look at.


It's fairly well featured with different drawing tools. The art lessons are ok, but they don't attempt to give feedback, you just have to judge yourself how well you're following along.


A certified DS classic.
