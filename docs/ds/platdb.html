Screenshots

Notes

It's another Professor Layton game! Fundamentally, it's more of the same, but it's more of the same that I quite like.


The story is very much on a theme with the first one, with a new silly twist ending.


The puzzles were mostly good and felt fun. There were a few too many of the "push blocks around to free one from an exit" which I am quite sick of. Also this particular game loved really challenging spot the difference puzzles across the two screens.
