Pokemon Trozei!

  • Complete on 2023-05-27

  • 3 / 5

  • Release Date: Mar 6, 2006

  • Meta Score: 74

Screenshots

Notes

A pretty fun arcade-style puzzle game. Match-4, a stunning twist from match 3. Pretty much the whole game is setting up combos of 4-3-2+ matches to clear the whole board, then waiting for it to fill up a gain.


Has a story mode with an ending. Gets quite frustrating towards the end as the levels are very long, the board fills up fast, and any loss of concentration will pretty much have you fail. Boss fights have weird mechanics and can be very frustrating. Randomly refilling the target and blacking out the screen mainly.


Fairly fun, not as good as Meteos for example.
