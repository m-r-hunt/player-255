Notes

This stalled me out for a long time because I wasn't feeling it. I tried to get back into it but it's just not very fun.


It's a top down action game. Core gameplay reminds me most of DS Zelda games - touch screen action adventure. But the structure of the game is a level based action game. Follows the story of the original Dragon Ball (not Z) and kid Goku.


Core fighting gameplay gets repetetive very fast and you have to fight dozens of the same enemies that take many hits to kill. Boss fights were a little better. Definitely got sick of it fast.
