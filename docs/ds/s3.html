Notes

I thought this was going to be good as it has a decent score and other Spider-Man games have been decent (if not amazing).


It kinda sucks actually.


Heavily touch based controls that add no value, only make the game worse to play.


Early on in the game it hit me with "Enemies with weapons are hard to attack or web" and offered no solution to how to deal with them. Then put several in the way as mandatory obstacles. What am I supposed to do?


In general enemies are few but each one has a ton of health. I think it wants you to make a lot of use of webs and attacks like throws and juggles but the crappy controls make that suck.


Ended up putting it down.
