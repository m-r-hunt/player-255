Reasonable platformer tie in with a 3d action/adventure game on home consoles. Technically fine if a little uninspired. Only real gripe is the birds that appear from nowhere flying at your face as a reaction test.


Stopped playing due to losing a significant amount of progress. The game apparently autosaves (no manual saving) but not very frequently. Maybe you need to explicitly quit? In any case, it's not good enough for me to replay a whole world+.
