Notes

This is another odd one. It wants to be a run and gun platformer like Metal Slug, but fails to capture anything that makes that genre fun. Controls are stiff and awkward, your default gun is very bad and you don't get a ton of ammo for other guns. Gameplay is slowed down by awkward platforming and somewhat mazelike levels. Didn't enjoy it.
