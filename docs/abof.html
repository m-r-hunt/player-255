Astro Boy: Omega Factor

Complete on 2018-7-15

5 / 5

Release Date: Aug 18, 2004

Meta Score: 85

Notes

Fantastic side scrolling platformer/beat em up. Great graphics (with occasional lag), big bosses, and a decent amount of content. Like a Mega Man game but with more bombast and a much less annoying continue system. One of the best games I've played on the GBA.
