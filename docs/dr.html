Drome Racers

Complete on 2018-7-28

3 / 5

Release Date: Jun 19, 2003

Meta Score: 73

Notes

Surprisingly good 3D racer, based on a Lego franchise. 3D graphics aren't amazing, but you can actually tell what's going on, unlike in some other GBA 3D titles. Gameplay is decent, you have a drag race warmup followed by a racetrack or offroad race. You can upgrade your cars in career mode. Computer racers seemed to be fairly uncompetetive though. Pretty decent fun actually. Tracks are a bit samey, and ice/frozen tracks are annoying. Seemingly no credits, but I did get a shot of my full trophy collection.
