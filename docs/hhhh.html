Notes

Yes, my second mega-cute hamster game. This one is more of an adventure, where you go around trying to reunite hamster couples that are being split up by a hamster devil. Gameplay is mainly learning and then using "hamchat" verbs to do various actions. Often quite illogical and requires a guide to get through it. Still, didn't totally hate it.
