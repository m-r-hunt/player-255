The original Zelda: we have a lot to thank it for, including some of the best GBA games. However, in my opinion it has aged very badly and I do not enjoy trying to play it. Clunky gameplay, a confusing overworld map, and cryptic badly-translated NPC messages. Had a quick play but not going to slog through it.
